BIOGRAPHY

Murlidhar Devidas Amte, popularly known as Baba Amte, (26 December 1914 - 9 February 2008) was an Indian social worker and social activist known particuarly for his wor for the rehabilitation and empowerment of people suffering from leprosy.

The great social reformer Baba Amte dedicated his entire life to serving humanity and he moved forward with th motto "Work Build; Charity Destroys". Baba formed Anandwan to serve these people.
